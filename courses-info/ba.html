Bachelor of Arts

Description

A Bachelor of Arts is a college or university degree available in various subjects. Each program covers standard general education requirements on liberal arts and humanities topics and then allows you to major in a particular specialization of your choice.It is one of the most common undergraduate degrees in the United States. Earning your BA typically takes about four to five years of full-time study, and you'll often study topics in the arts, humanities, and social sciences.

Faculty Profile

The course of bachelor of Arts (B.A) is being offered in the institution under acdemics scheme and regulated through the Higher education institute sociaty regd.Under cooperative sociaty act 1960. The durationn of B.A degree course is the three years spread over in six semsester and is affiliated to Himachal pradesh university shimla. Eligibility Conditions

A candidate who has passed 10+2 or equivalent examination can get admission in the Ist year of three years degree course of B.A. if he/she fulfills the following eligibility conditions:
1. An applicant must not be more than 23 years of age (25 yrs. in case of girl students) . The upper age limit is relax able by three years for SC/ST candidates.
2. Minimum percentage of marks for admission to the Ist year of B.A. is 33%.
3. If a candidate intends to take up Mathematics in B.A. I he/she should have studied Mathematics at 10+2 level and secured either 45% marks in Mathematics or 50% marks in aggregate
