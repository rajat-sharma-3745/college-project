Bachelor of Business Administration

Description

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is one of the most popular undergraduate degree programmes for students who want to pursue a career in management after completing Class 12th. This course provides understanding related to the administration of the business and managing the business operations. BBA can be pursued by the students from all the three major streams namely Science, Arts, and Commerce.

Faculty Profile

The course of bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is being offered in the institution under self-financing scheme and regulated through the Higher education institute society regd.Under cooperative society act 2006. The duration of BBA degree course is the three years spread over in six semester and is affiliated to Himachal pradesh university shimla. The basic objective start this courses in this institution is to impart quality education in management to the candidates admitted for the course to build their career in the business paradigm.

Eligibility Conditions

Candidates who have passed 10+2 examination from H.P.Board/CBSE/ICSE or any other board, cosidered equivalent to 10+2,by the Himachal Pradesh University,Shimla-5,with 40% marks(35% marks for SC/ST category),shall be eligible. As per H.P University rule 75% seats will be filled out the candidate who have passed thrie 10+2 examination form the school situated in Himachal Pradesh irrespectives of the board .Remaining 25% seats will be filled on all indias basis .Other reservation rules are as per H.P.University .
