Bachelor of Science

Description

A Bachelor of Science (B.S.) is an undergraduate degree awarded to those who study mathematics or a natural science. Most Bachelor of Science degree programs require students to take between 120 and 133 semester hours, or between 40 and 42 classes. It usually takes students four years to earn a B.S. degree. These programs provide a focused education in a students' major and often require fewer general education courses than do other undergraduate degree programs.

Faculty Profile

The course of bachelor of Science (BSC) is being offered in the institution under academics scheme and regulated through the Higher education institute society regd.Under cooperative society act 1960. The duration of BSC degree course is the three years spread over in six semester and is affiliated to Himachal pradesh university shimla. The basic objective start this courses in this institution is to imaprt quality education in science to the candidates admitted for the course to build their career in the science paradigm.

Eligibility Conditions

A candidate who has passed 10+2 or equivalent examination can get admission in the Ist year of three years degree course of B.Sc. if he/she fulfills the following eligibility conditions:
1. An applicant must not be more than 23 years of age (25 yrs. in case of girl students) on 1-07-11. The upper age limit is relax able by three years for SC/ST candidates.
2. Minimum percentage of marks for admission to the Ist year of B.Sc. is 45%.
3. An applicant seeking admission in B.Sc. I year must have studied Physics, Chemistry and Biology/Mathematics at 10+2 level.
