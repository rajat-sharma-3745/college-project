Our Facilities

World Class Library

A library is a place where books and sources of information are stored. They make it easier for people to get access to them for various purposes. Libraries are very helpful and economical too.

Large Play Ground

A playground is an area used by students to play in. We usually have equipment such as slides, swings and seesaws for children to play on. They are usually outside, but we have incampus.

Special Education

It is the practice of educating students in a way that accommodates their special needs. This involves systematically monitored arrangement of teaching procedures, accessible settings

College Hostel

The hostel offer scopes like hygienic washrooms, clean and tidy rooms, good food, recreational space, and playground for outdoor games along with strict vigilance. Other necessities like round-the-clock internet connection are also present.

Placement

Campus placement is a necessary feature of our college. But the training around it constitutes the facilities that you can expect from such colleges. A vigilant training and placement cell is a must-have facility within our college.
